We have a help me find service which exist to help our users in a case where they couldn't find the exact property they need for a particular purpose. To use the help me find service, just contact us with the provided email and ensure to specify the relevant details of the property you need.

Cost Of Our Services

For now, listing and locating properties on this platform is free of charge.

Furthermore, the amount you pay as commitment fee is non-refundable but will be deducted from the amount you are required to pay as commission after buying or renting the property in point.

You may pay a commitment fee and the property deal may not be concluded at the end due to many factors such as: no needing the said property anymore, not having enough money for the property found for you etc. if that is the case, you can be rest assured that we have no interest in cheating you out of you already paid commitment fee.

We have a commitment to judiciously utilize every commitment fee because we have high respect for integrity and we care that it's your hard earned money, therefore we give details on how your money was spent and will add any remaining balance to your account with us.
